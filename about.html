About Shipmate

Reliable freight, built on a foundation of precision.

Shipmate Logistics is a pan-India freight network with a fleet of 1000+ vehicles across six regional hubs. We move cargo with discipline, transparency, and a relentless focus on on-time delivery.

Our approach

Built for reliability, from the ground up

Shipmate Logistics was created to bring a new standard of reliability to Indian freight. From day one, our operations have been built around one principle: cargo arrives when we say it will, not when it happens to get there.

We designed a hub-and-spoke network that reduces transit times and increases route density, while our preventive maintenance program ensures every vehicle is road-ready before each trip. Technology is not an afterthought—live GPS tracking, temperature logs, and digital proof of delivery are standard on every shipment.

Our team of experienced operations planners and trained drivers works together to anticipate challenges before they become delays. The result is a logistics service that clients trust for consistency, transparency, and accountability.
